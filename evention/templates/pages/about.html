About Evention

Hi, I'm Eric. I made a thing. It's pretty alright. Follow it on GitHub.

How it works

First you should search for performers and build up a list of your favourites.

Then you should compile a list of your favourite cities and places you want to visit.

Finally, hop over to the events page and see what your favourite bands are up to, and when they'll be somewhere you want to visit. Want an excuse to go on vacation? There it is!

Planned features

In no particular order, here are some of the things that will be coming next to Evention:

Sports.

Importing favourite artists from Spotify and other services.

Adding your planned trips and get information on relevant events happening while you're in a particular area.

Deals on event tickets and flights delivered to you via the site or email.
